About Labour connect Hub

Labour Connect Hub (LCH) connect individuals to the labour market. It provides a quick access to available labour, connecting businesses and individuals using the latest technologies.

If need the service of a plumber, carpenter, driver, developers, architect, etc, just with a push of a button on your mobile phone from the comfort of your home, you can see businesses around you and reach out to them, any time any day.

We take away the hassles to reaching small businesses and larger enterprises in times of emergency.
